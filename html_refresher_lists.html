Kodama Spirits

These beings can be found in the northern forests after dusk. It is said they bring good fortune to lucky wanderers.

Tips to increase the chance of encountering them:

  • They love the smell of durans.
    1. Place duran in a mesh.
    2. Hook mesh on a rope attached to a stick.
    3. Wave around the duran while walking to let scent surround your vicinity.
  • Hum soft melodies.
  • They don't like a lot of commotion.
